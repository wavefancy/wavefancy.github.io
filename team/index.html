Our Team

We are a group of highly motivated and creative people, discovering and innovating together to make precision medicine a reality through the catalytic power of big genomic data, statistical genetics, and artificial intelligence.

Wallace (Minxian) Wang is a principal investigator at Beijing Institute of Genomics, Chinese Academy of Sciences, China National Center for Bioinformation. His lab is interested in disease risk variants and genes profiling by genome-wide association studies, genetic risk prediction, precision medicine and making large-scale biomedical data sounds through statistical genetic modeling and artificial intelligence, with the ultimate goal of assisting in guiding precise disease prevention, leading to healthier lifestyles, and in the discovery of new therapeutic targets.

Minxian Wallace Wang
Principal Investigator, Group Leader

Dr. Fei Wang is a Research Assistant Professor in the Wang lab at Beijing Institute of Genomics, Chinese Academy of Sciences (China National Center for Bioinformation). She completed her Ph.D. in animal genetics from Nanjing Agricultural University. She completed her postdoctoral training in the medical school of Université Catholique de Louvain, Belgium. Dr. Fei Wang is skilled in resolving biological questions by conducting omics and cell molecular experiments. She is interested in disease risk prediction by integratively analyzing GWAS data with multi-omics data, aiming to research and reveal the underlying genetic mechanism for disease etiology and precise disease prediction.
